How to Play

  1. Select your difficulty level: Padawan, Jedi Knight, or Jedi Master
  2. Solve the equation: [num1] ? [num2] = [num3]
  3. Choose the correct operation (+, -, ×) before time runs out
  4. Earn more points for harder difficulties and faster solves
  5. Compete for the top spot on the leaderboard!
